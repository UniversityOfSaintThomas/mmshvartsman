MATH 114-01:  Course Goals
Instructor: Misha Shvartsman 

General Goals:
1. Gaining factual knowledge (terminology and methods)
2. Learning to apply course material (problem solving)

Subject Goals:
1. To learn and to understand how methods of calculus, differentiation and integration techniques, can be
used in computing various geometric and physical quantities.
2. To develop skills in evaluating limits of sequences, series sums, integrals, and learn how to formulate 
and solve related applied problems
