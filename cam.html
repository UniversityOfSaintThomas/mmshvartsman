The Center for Applied Mathematics (CAM) is soliciting
Applications from UST faculty for Summer 2024 Research Projects

 

Please do not share this application with students. They will have their own application to complete in February. 

 

Please email your completed application to Misha Shvartsman by 5:00 p.m. on February 9, 2024. We will review applications and notify you if your project is funded by late February. We will then have a time frame
for students to complete an application to be a CAM summer researcher from late February to late March.
During this time, we will also have a CAM event on Thursday, February 29 during convo hour for students
to learn more about the summer research program and projects. If your project is selected, we will send you all of the student applications. In mid to late March, you will notify us which student(s) would be your top choice(s). You will have time to interview students in early April. We will make the final decision and notify students in Mid-April who will have until late April to accept or decline.


Here is a rough timeline of the process: 

Present – February 9:  Faculty submit project proposals. 

February 9 – late February: Decisions on submitted proposals. 

Late February – late March: Student application window (CAM event about the summer reserach program and projects on Thursday, February 29 during convo hour)

Early April: Faculty review applications and interview students on their own.

Mid-April:  Faculty send me their top pick and we make the final decisions on student applicants. 

Mid to Late April: Student acceptance window. 

June 10 - August 16: Project window. 

Introductory Meeting: June 10

Introductory research talk : June 25

Symposium: August 8

Report due: August 16

We will have a competitive faculty stipend. We also are increasing the hourly wage for students to $16/hour.
