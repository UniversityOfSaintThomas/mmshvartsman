Homework Assignments: I will assign homework every Wednesday and it will be due next week.
Assignments will usually involve theoretical work and may include some programming. You will have one
week to complete these assignments. Sometimes you may have to start working on an assignment before
the specific topic is fully explored in class. In that case I will clearly indicate reading that has to be done to
complete your work efficiently. You are encouraged to work together on homework assignments, but each
student must write up and turn in their own work. Even if you collaborate on homework, please learn to write
your own solutions. So, the solutions and codes that you turn in have to be completed by you and may not be
copied without attribution to a source. More often than not your first attempt at writing a program will
not work (i.e. it will contain 'bugs"). Be sure to start working on your homework assignments early enough so
that you have sufficient time to fix the bugs and get your programs to work correctly.
