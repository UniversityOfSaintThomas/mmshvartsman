MATH 101-01-02: Course Goals
 Instructor: Misha Shvartsman 

General Goals:
 1. Gaining factual knowledge (terminology and methods)
 2. Learning to apply course material (problem solving)

Subject Goals:
1. Cultivate a positive mathematical mindset by building a strong foundation of mathematics.
All of us come from different mathematical backgrounds and together we will complete activities
and assignments to strength our knowledge and gain confidence in mathematics.
2. Gain factual knowledge of finite mathematics that is critical for further study of mathematics,
economics, business, statistics, and accounting. To achieve this, we will study financial mathematics
and we will also study probability, statistics, normal and binomial distributions.
3. Learn fundamental principles and theories of finite mathematics. To achieve this, we will study
linear equations and functions. In addition, we will study counting techniques and calculate probabilities
for various situations.
4. Learn to apply finite mathematics to improve thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making skills.
To achieve this, we will learn how to use matrices to organize and easily interpret data. We will learn
techniques to make wise decisions by maximizing or minimizing quantities which are important
in good business practices.
