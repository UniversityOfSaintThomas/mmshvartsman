MATH 200-01-02: Course Goals
Instructor: Misha Shvartsman 

General Goals:
1. Gaining factual knowledge (terminology and methods)
2. Learning to apply course material (problem solving)

Subject Goals:

1. To learn and to understand how geometry of three-dimensional space can be described using vectors and vector functions
2. To develop skills in evaluating limits, derivatives, extremal properties and integrals for the functions of several variables;
that portion will extend ideas you learned in the calculus of one variable (covered in MATH 113 and MATH 114) to a multivariable setting.
3. To learn and to understand concepts and methods of vector analysis
4. To learn and to understand concepts of line and surface integrals in the context of vector analysis
5. To learn and to understand concepts of curl and divergence 
6. To learn and to understand Green's, Divergence and Stokes' Theorems
