Topics of the Course:   
1. Computer Arithmetic
2. Numerical Solution of Scalar Equations
3. Review of Matrix Algebra
4. Gaussian Elimination
5. Inner Products and Norms
6. Eigenvalues and Singular Values
7. Iterative Methods for Linear Systems
8. Numerical Computation of Eigenvalues
9. Numerical Solution of Algebraic Systems
10. Numerical Solution of Ordinary Differential Equations
11. Numerical Solution of the Heat and Wave Equations
12. Minimization
13. Approximation and Interpolation
14. The Finite Element Method

Homework Assignments: I will assign homework every class and it will be due next class. Homework
solutions have to be written neatly and clearly and should be submitted on Canvas by due date. Late submissions
will not be graded. Assignments will usually involve theoretical work and may include some programming. You are
encouraged to work together on homework assignments, but each student must write up and turn in their own work.
Even if you collaborate on homework, please learn to write your own solutions. So, the solutions and codes that you
turn in have to be completed by you and may not be copied without attribution to a source.
