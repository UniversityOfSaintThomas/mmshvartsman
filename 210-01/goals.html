MATH 210-01: Course Goals
Instructor: Misha Shvartsman 

General Goals:
1. Gaining factual knowledge (terminology and methods)
2. Learning to apply course material (problem solving)

Subject Goals:

1. To learn principles and foundations of modeling with differential equations and systems
2. To learn and understand analytic methods of solving differential equations and systems
3. To learn and understand graphical methods of solving differential equations and systems
4. To learn and understand numerical methods of solving differential equations and systems
5. To learn methods of analysis of nonlinear differential equations and systems of nonlinear
differential equations
